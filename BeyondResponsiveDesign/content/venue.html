Location

DDD East Anglia for 2014 is taking place at the West Road Concert Hall, located on the edge of Cambridge city centre.

Directions

The address of West Road is:

West Road Concert Hall
11 West Road
Cambridge
CB3 9DP


Getting here by car

Please note that there is no parking available on site, however there are several car parks in the city centre within walking distance of the venue. Alternatively, parking is available at Madingley Road Park and Ride site, with a bus towards Cambridge. You will need to get off the bus at the Bridge Street stop and walk to the venue (approx. 15 mins).


Getting here by train

Cambridge railway station is located a few miles away from the venue. Buses can be caught outside the station to the city centre (take the Citi 1 towards Arbury, Citi 3 towards Fison Road, Citi 7 towards Cambridge, or Citi 8 towards Cottenham). You will need to get off the bus at Drummer Street bus station and walk to the venue (approx. 20 mins).


Bus Information

West Road Concert Hall is not located directly on a bus route, but you may need to take a bus to get to the venue. MyBusTrip is a free mobile phone app that shows real-time bus information in the cambridge area.
